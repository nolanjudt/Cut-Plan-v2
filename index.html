🥘 Meal Prep Schedule (2× Weekly): Monday prep covers Tue–Fri training. Friday prep covers Sat–Mon training. Rest days use fresh, same-day cooking only.
Monday Meal Prep (~45 min) Covers Tue–Fri Training

1. Bake Chicken (22–25 min @ 375°F)

  • 2.5 lbs chicken breast on baking sheet
  • Season with salt, pepper, optional garlic powder
  • → Portion into 151g cooked blocks (use kitchen scale)
  • → Store in separate containers for Tue/Thu/Fri lunch meals

2. Brown Ground Beef

  • 1.5 lbs 90% lean ground beef in large skillet over medium-high heat
  • Break apart with wooden spoon, cook ~8–10 min until no pink remains
  • Drain thoroughly in fine mesh strainer to remove fat
  • → Portion into 140g cooked blocks
  • → Store for Tue/Thu/Fri dinner meals

3. Cook Jasmine Rice

  • 120g dry jasmine rice (1 cup uncooked) in 2 cups water
  • Bring to boil, reduce heat to low, cover, simmer 15 min
  • Let rest 5 min, fluff with fork
  • → Yields ~360g cooked rice (Tue/Thu/Fri = 115g cooked each)
  • → Divide into 3 lunch containers

⚠️ DO NOT batch vegetables. Store broccoli, string beans raw in crisper drawer. Cook fresh daily 5–10 min before eating (air-fry broccoli 380°F 6–8 min, boil string beans 4 min).

Friday Meal Prep (~45 min) Covers Sat–Mon Training

1. Bake Chicken (20–22 min @ 375°F)

  • 2.0 lbs chicken breast on baking sheet
  • Season with salt, pepper, optional garlic powder
  • → Portion into 151g cooked blocks
  • → Store for Sat/Mon lunch meals

2. Brown Ground Beef

  • 1.0 lb 90% lean ground beef in skillet
  • Cook ~6–8 min, break apart, drain thoroughly
  • → Portion into 140g cooked blocks
  • → Store for Sat/Mon dinner meals

3. Cook Jasmine Rice

  • 80g dry jasmine rice (⅔ cup) in 1.3 cups water
  • Same cooking method as Monday (boil, simmer 15 min, rest 5 min)
  • → Divide into 2 lunch containers (Sat/Mon)

⚠️ Asparagus: Store raw Friday, air-fry fresh Sunday morning ONLY (prevents sogginess). Broccoli and other vegetables stored raw in crisper until use.

Daily Fresh Cooking (Each Training Day ~10–15 min) Cook Before Eating

🥔 Yellow Potatoes (Pre-Training Meal 4 @ 12 PM)

  • Weigh 170g raw yellow potatoes (put on scale, add water as needed)
  • Cut into 1–1.5 inch chunks (skins on for faster cook)
  • Air-fry @ 400°F for 18 minutes, shake basket halfway
  • → Yields 135g cooked (measured after cooking)
  • Alternative: Can prep night-before and reheat morning-of in fridge (microwave 2–3 min)

🥦 Broccoli (Training Days)

  • Weigh 4.5 oz fresh broccoli (chop into florets)
  • Air-fry @ 380°F for 6–8 minutes until bright green and tender-crisp
  • Do immediately before eating (takes ~8 min total including air-fry)

🫘 String Beans (Tue Training Dinner)

  • Weigh 4.0 oz fresh string beans (trim ends)
  • Boil in salted water for exactly 4 minutes
  • Drain, season with salt/pepper, optional 1 tsp butter

🍃 Asparagus (Sun Rest Day Only — Fresh Same-Day)

  • Weigh 200g fresh asparagus (trim woody ends)
  • Air-fry @ 390°F for 7 minutes
  • Do Sunday morning ONLY — never batch (becomes soggy)

Rest Day Meals (Wed, Sat): Never batch-cook. Scramble eggs fresh (10 min), make tuna salad fresh (5 min), roast broccoli fresh (8 min). Total: 15 min per rest day, all fresh.
