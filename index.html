🥘 Prep Cadence: Monday prep covers Tue–Fri training meals. Friday prep covers Sat–Mon. Rest day meals (Wed, Sat) are always fresh, never batched.
Monday Prep (~45 min)Covers Tue–Fri
1. Bake Chicken (375°F · 22–25 min)
  • 2.5 lbs chicken breast on baking sheet, season with salt/pepper/garlic powder
  • Portion into 151g cooked blocks (kitchen scale)
  • Store in 3 separate containers → Tue, Thu, Fri lunches
2. Brown Ground Beef
  • 1.5 lbs 90% lean ground beef in large skillet, medium-high heat ~8–10 min
  • Drain thoroughly in fine mesh strainer
  • Portion into 140g cooked blocks → Tue, Thu, Fri dinners
3. Cook Jasmine Rice
  • 120g dry rice in 2 cups water → boil, reduce, cover, simmer 15 min, rest 5 min
  • Divide into 3 lunch containers (115g cooked each)
⚠️ DO NOT batch vegetables. Store broccoli and string beans raw in crisper. Cook fresh daily (air-fry broccoli 380°F 6–8 min, boil string beans 4 min).
Friday Prep (~45 min)Covers Sat–Mon
1. Bake Chicken (375°F · 20–22 min)
  • 2.0 lbs chicken breast → portion into 151g cooked blocks
  • Store for Sat and Mon lunches
2. Brown Ground Beef
  • 1.0 lb 90% lean ground beef → cook 6–8 min, drain thoroughly
  • Portion into 140g cooked blocks → Sat and Mon dinners
3. Cook Jasmine Rice
  • 80g dry rice → same cooking method, divide into 2 lunch containers
⚠️ Asparagus: Store raw in crisper. Air-fry @ 390°F for 7 min fresh on Sunday morning only — never batch.
Daily Fresh Cooking (~10–15 min each day)
🥔 Yellow Potatoes (Pre-Workout Meal · 12 PM)
  • Weigh 170g raw, cut into 1–1.5 inch chunks (skin on)
  • Air-fry @ 400°F · 18 min, shake halfway → yields 135g cooked
  • Can prep night-before and microwave 2–3 min morning-of
🥦 Broccoli (Training Days · Pre-Lunch)
  • Weigh 4.5 oz fresh broccoli florets
  • Air-fry @ 380°F · 6–8 min until bright green and tender-crisp
  • Cook immediately before eating, never batch
🫘 String Beans (Tue Dinner)
  • Weigh 4.0 oz fresh, trim ends
  • Boil in salted water exactly 4 min
  • Drain, season with salt and pepper
🌿 Asparagus (Sun Rest Day Only)
  • Weigh 200g fresh, trim woody ends
  • Air-fry @ 390°F · 7 min
  • Sunday morning only — never batch, becomes soggy
Rest Day Meals (Wed, Sat): Always cook fresh. Eggs ~10 min, tuna salad ~5 min, broccoli ~8 min. Total: 15 min per rest day.
